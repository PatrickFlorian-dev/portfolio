  • 2009

    Get off my mountain

    In the middle of winter driving up through Keystone, Colorado at around 3am to avoid traffic I got caught by a blizzard. While I tried stopping my mustang after miscalculating a turn, I narrowly avoided falling off a mountain by a matter of roughly 10 feet. If it weren't for the power of the mustang;s acceleration I'd be a goner.

  • 2012

    Bear-ly lunch

    I love the smokey mountains and so do the bears. While hiking in the early morning I got this unusal feeling of somebody or in this case something watching me. I turn around and I see a brown bear, I turned facing it and kept walking backwards away from it and luckily it lost interest, phew.

  • 2016

    What do you mean I missed the tram?

    While in Sweeden I saw a mountain and a tram on rails going up to it. Turns out it was a nice but cold spring day and I decided to take the tram up there. After a long day I decided to take a nap and I wake up at sunset only to noice the tram is no longer in service until 10am the next morning. One of the coldest nights I've slept through.

  • 2019

    Sharks .... wait SHARKS!!?

    Back when I lived in Clearwater I was just taking a morning kayak trip on Treasure Island when suddenly I feel a bump on my kayak. I thought it was a dolphin or manatee as I normally see until I see the dreaded fin you see in movies. I don't think I ever kayaked faster in my life!
